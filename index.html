Om mig

Om mig

Systematically conceiving, designing, implementing, programming and evaluating prototypes of digital media products (including their software) targeted at specific users, e.g., apps, web pages, games, audio-visual installations, tangible interfaces, virtual reality experiences, augmented reality technologies, etc. Analysing, applying, and developing theories and technologies behind digital media products and their software. Designing and carrying out iterative development processes that generate knowledge about users and feeding this into product designs to optimise the user experience in a given context. Operating effectively as a member of a multi-disciplinary research & development team in collaboration with clients and representative users. Designing and carrying out scientifically sound experiments to evaluate media products and prototypes, as well as analysing and communicating the results thereof.
